D'Wallet

Description

Problem: Have you ever had to leave a public transport as soon as you enter just because your card isn't recharged? Have you ever need to just recharge your mobile to make a quick call? Or do you feel like buying online comes with a risk and it should be cheaper?

D'Wallet is a digital wallet that allows you to use you're money safely and quickly.

  • The user will be able to use this digital money to buy anything online and recharge you mobile and your transport cards.
  • The user can charge his wallet through a bank transfer or by linking his bank accounts into the wallet account.
  • The user can check his transaction with the wallet.
  • The main advantage of using D'Wallet is that there are several exclusive sales and the user will get 5% discount on several online shops.

Target Users

D'Wallet is an app for everyone who wants to save both money and time. Although the user must be at ease with a computer and with the internet.

Project Goal

The main goal of D'Wallet is to be there in those moments that you just can't use an ATM and using your bank's web application is just takes forever but you still need to recharge/buy something quickly and easily.

You can do all of that with a couple of clicks with D'Wallet. By holding virtual money ready to be spent on demand, D'Wallet can easily complete those task for you.

Competition

PayPal: with PayPal a user can have a virtual wallet that can be used to buy online. Although you can't recharge your transport card/mobile directly within the app. Also, PayPal doesn't offer any kind of discout and/or sale. At least not as often as D'Wallet do.
